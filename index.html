Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our hydrogen, fuel cell, and CCUS technology.

What does Shakti Photon Solutions make?

We are an Indian deep-tech company that manufactures on-site green hydrogen generators (PEM, AEM, and Alkaline electrolyzers from 0.01 kW to 100 kW), fuel cell systems (1W to 400 kW), and CCUS systems that convert CO₂ into valuable chemicals. We are headquartered at IIT Madras Research Park, Chennai, and trusted by 9+ premier institutions.

What is a PEM electrolyzer and who makes them in India?

A PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolyzer splits water into hydrogen and oxygen using electricity. It produces 99.999% pure hydrogen on-site, eliminating cylinder logistics. Shakti Photon Solutions manufactures PEM electrolyzers in India, alongside AEM and Alkaline systems — for industrial, research, and backup power applications.

What is CCUS and do you do carbon capture in India?

CCUS stands for Carbon Capture, Utilization and Storage. We build electrochemical CO₂ reduction systems that convert captured carbon dioxide into valuable chemicals — CO, Formic Acid, and Ethylene — using renewable electricity. This closes the carbon loop and helps industries reach net zero. Our CCUS systems scale from lab to pilot plant.

Are you a MEA manufacturer in India?

Yes. Shakti Photon Solutions manufactures Membrane Electrode Assemblies (MEAs) for PEM and AEM electrolyzers and fuel cells. We also offer MEA recycling services and provide Equipment as a Service (EaaS) access to our ultrasonic spray nozzle and hot press for custom MEA fabrication by researchers and startups.

How do you help companies reach net zero?

We are a net-zero enabler. We provide the hardware that decarbonises energy and industry: green hydrogen generators replace fossil-fuel-based hydrogen, fuel cells replace diesel generators, and CCUS systems capture and valorise industrial CO₂. We also offer full EPC integration — combining hydrogen, carbon, and renewable energy into one turnkey clean energy project.

What is Equipment as a Service (EaaS)?

Our EaaS programme lets researchers and startups access expensive lab equipment by the session — without buying the machines. We offer RF Magnetron Sputtering, Ultrasonic Spray Nozzle (catalyst/MEA coating), Hot Press (MEA bonding), and 3D Printing (rapid prototyping). Ideal for PhD researchers, hydrogen startups, and small-batch MEA manufacturers across India.
