Our Mission

Make Green Hydrogen Accessible to Every Indian Business

India's hydrogen mission calls for 5 million tonnes of green hydrogen by 2030. But the technology gap between global R&D and Indian industry is vast. Shakti Photon Solutions was founded to close that gap — by scientists who've actually built these systems at the world's best labs.

We believe that on-site hydrogen generation will transform Indian manufacturing, transportation, and energy — just as solar disrupted power generation. Our mission is to make that transformation accessible to every Indian business, university, and research lab.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

2022

Shakti Photon Solutions Founded

Company incorporated at IIT Madras Research Park, Chennai. First hydrogen generator prototype completed by the founding team.

2023

First IIT Delhi Installation

Successfully installed and commissioned first commercial PEM electrolyzer at IIT Delhi. Expanded to 9+ institutional customers including NIT Warangal and TIFR Hyderabad.

2024

CLIMAFIX 2024 Finalist — IIT Madras

Selected as a finalist at India's Largest Climate Startup Summit at IIT Madras, hosted by The Energy Consortium and EAI. Opened Guntur and Mohali offices.

2025

Green Hydrogen Summit 2025

Presented at SRM Green Hydrogen Summit 2025 alongside government officials. Launched Equipment as a Service offering. Expanding to 100 kW industrial electrolyzer deployments.
