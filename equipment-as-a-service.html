Ultrasonic Spray Nozzle

Achieve ultrafine, uniform catalyst ink deposition on Nafion membranes, carbon substrates, and GDEs. Our ultrasonic spray system is the industry standard for reproducible MEA fabrication — eliminating the non-uniformity problems of traditional doctor-blade or brush coating methods.

Equipment Specifications
Atomisation Ultrasonic (25–120 kHz)
Droplet Size 10–80 μm
Compatible Inks Nafion, Pt/C, IrO₂, catalyst inks
Substrate Heating Up to 120 °C
Use Cases
MEA catalyst layer coating Nafion membrane coating GDE carbon cloth deposition Ionomer spraying AEM electrode fabrication

Bond membrane electrode assemblies (MEAs) under precise temperature and pressure conditions. Our hot press is the critical final step in MEA fabrication — ensuring proper catalyst-membrane interface, which directly determines your electrolyzer or fuel cell performance.
